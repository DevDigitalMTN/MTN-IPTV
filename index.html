MTN SYR

MTN IPTV app

It is a digital television viewing service via Internet Protocol.
Subscribers of MTN ADSL will be able to benefit from the MTN IPTV service on the television screen only currently through the ADSL line.
The service mainly includes: live broadcast channels, movies, series, music, Quran, YouTube.

هي خدمة مشاهدة التلفزيون الرقمي عبر برتوكول الانترنت.
